📅 Our Story

Passionate About the World. Devoted to You.

🌍 Who We Are

Born from a Love of Authentic Travel

WanderLux was founded in 2006 by Elena and Marco Reyes, a couple who gave up corporate careers to pursue what mattered most — showing the world's beauty to the people who were ready to truly see it.

What began as a one-room office in Barcelona has grown into an award-winning travel agency with specialists on six continents. But our core belief has never changed: travel should be meaningful, well-crafted, and deeply personal.

We don't sell holidays. We design journeys that become part of who you are.

✨ Our Values

The Principles That Guide Every Journey

🌎

Authenticity First

We reject the formulaic. Every itinerary is crafted to reflect where you are, not what a template says you should see.

🌿

Responsible Travel

We partner with locally-owned operators, support conservation projects, and offset the carbon footprint of every trip we plan.

🤝

Human Connection

Travel is about people — the ones you meet, the communities you visit, and the relationships you build with places.

🔥

Obsessive Detail

We sweat the small stuff so you don't have to. From your airport transfer to the flowers in your room — nothing is left to chance.

🕐 Our History

Eighteen Years of Extraordinary Journeys

2006

Founded in Barcelona

Elena and Marco Reyes open WanderLux in a small Barcelona office with a big vision and twelve clients.

2009

First International Award

Named "Best Boutique Travel Agency" at the European Travel Awards. Our Patagonia expedition wins widespread acclaim.

2013

Asia Pacific Expansion

We open our Tokyo and Bali offices, bringing authentic local expertise to our Japan and Southeast Asia programmes.

2017

10,000 Travellers Milestone

We celebrate sending our ten-thousandth traveller — a family on their first-ever safari in Kenya.

2021

Carbon Neutral Commitment

WanderLux becomes fully carbon neutral, partnering with three reforestation projects across South America and Southeast Asia.

2024

Today

Over 12,500 travellers served, specialists on six continents, and the same passion that started it all on day one.
